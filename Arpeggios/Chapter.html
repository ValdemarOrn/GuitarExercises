Chapter 3: Arpeggios

This chapter starts off by showing some basic arpeggio shapes (Major, Minor, Major 7th, Minor 7th) and then continues on with a collection of arpeggio-based licks and lessons. Note that the picking pattern for these exercises is left up to the player to choose, yet economy and sweep picking techniques should be preferred.
