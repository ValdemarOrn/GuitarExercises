Dexterity 3 - Ascending Triplets

Attempt to accentuate the first note of each triplet, this adds a new dimension to the problem, as you have to vary your picking strength in sync with the beat. It also means alternating accented notes on downstrokes and upstrokes.
  • Variation 1 - Index, Middle and Ring finger variation
  • Variation 2 - Middle, Ring and pinky finger variation
