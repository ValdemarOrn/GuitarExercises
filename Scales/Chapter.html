Chapter 2: Scales

This chapter contains exercises based on the Major (Ionian), Minor (Aeolian), Pentatonic Major and Pentatonic Minor scales. The first exercises cover the basic scales, and the 5 box positions for each (as well as a 3 note per string pattern for the Ionian and Aeolian scales). Some exercises focus on memorizing the box shapes and notes of the scale, while others focus on note patterns or string picking.
